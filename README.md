# CueMaster Suite
## A collection of utilities for the experienced pool player
#### CueMaster: Vectors (Advanced Cueing Emulator)
An advanced cueing emulator that lets you explore angles, spin, and speed. Map cue ball paths, test position plays, and experiment before you risk it on the cloth.
#### CueMaster: Tempo
A simple, match-ready shot clock for solo drills or competitive sets. Keep play moving, build rhythm under time pressure, and cut out arguments over slow shots.
#### CueMaster: TrueLevel
Quickly check how true your table rolls. Verify level, spot problem areas, and know when a miss was you – not the slate. Saved presets available for Pro users.
#### CueMaster: Velocity (COMING SOON)
Measure and track your break speed using your phone’s sensors. Benchmark your power, compare breaks over time, and dial in a consistent, controlled smash. Saved presets available for Pro users.
